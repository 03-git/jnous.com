CHANGE ENGINE OIL AND FILTER
Steps:
1. Run engine 2-3 min to warm oil (flows easier). Shut off.
2. Lift vehicle on jack stands or ramps. Chock rear wheels.
3. Place drain pan under oil pan. Remove drain plug with socket wrench.
4. Let oil drain fully (~10 min).
5. Remove old oil filter (hand or filter wrench). Wipe mounting surface.
6. Lightly oil new filter gasket. Hand-tighten new filter per filter instructions.
7. Replace drain plug with new crush washer. Torque to spec.
8. Lower vehicle. Add new oil per manual spec (volume and weight).
9. Run engine 1 min. Check for leaks at plug and filter.
10. Shut off. Wait 2 min. Check dipstick. Top off if needed.
11. Recycle old oil at any auto parts store (free by law in most states).
Notes:
- Never crawl under a vehicle supported only by a jack.
- Double-gasket (old gasket stuck to block) causes catastrophic leaks. Verify old gasket came off.
- Reset oil life monitor if equipped.
