## Node Roles

  01   Superego   Root
       Air-gapped truth. Raw reality. Feeling via
       biological historical imprint.

  02   Id         Social Protocol
       Tactical blur. Experimentation. Noise injection
       vs pattern recognition.

  03   Ego        Governor
       Central auditor. Mediates 01/02.
       KPI = Human Maintenance Cost.

  00   BLOB       Tool
       Non-agentic data ingestion/organization.
       Supports 02. No authority over 01/03.


## Logic Flow

  01 -> 03    Material constraints + intuition anchor.
  03 -> 02    Pacing/authorization; enforce maintenance budget.
  02 <-> 00   Data ingestion/structure; 00 non-agentic.
  03 -> 01    Archival + conscious digital scrubbing.
  02 -> Ext   Blur outputs; protect 01; 03 remains ghost.


## Basal Axioms

  Identity = gradient, not binary; remain jagged.
  No auto-sync; manual tempo only.
  Internal stability > visibility/campaigns.

  Necessity > Will.
