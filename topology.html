## II. Node Roles

  01   Superego   Root
       Air-gapped sanctuary for immutable truth and raw
       reality. The source of Feeling, synthesizing
       victory/failure via biological feedback.

  02   Id         Curiosity
       Tactical blur. Handles curiosity and experimentation
       using noise injection to prevent pattern recognition.

  03   Ego        Governor
       Central auditor mediating traffic between Shadow
       and Root. KPI = Human Maintenance Cost.

  00   BLOB       Binary Large Object
       Non-agentic tool for data ingestion and organization.
       Supports Node 02 but lacks authority over Node 01.


## III. Logic Flow

  01 -> 03    Material constraints + biological intuition.
              The anchor.

  03 -> 02    Pacing and authorization. Tactical experiments
              do not exceed the human maintenance budget.

  02 <-> 00   Data ingestion and organization. 00 is a tool.

  03 -> 01    Closes the loop. Intentional archival of anchor
              material and conscious scrubbing of digital
              clutter. Feedback that builds internal capacity.

  02 -> Ext   Blur outputs to obfuscate public tracing.
              01 is high-contrast. 03 remains a ghost.


## IV. Basal Axioms

  Sfumato Defense
       Identity is a gradient, not a binary. Remain jagged
       to resist profiling.

  Anti-Sync Decree
       Automatic syncing is a biological vulnerability.
       Data flows only via manual human tempo.

  Blur, Not Brand
       Prioritize internal stabilization and capacity
       management over public visibility or pre-defined
       campaigns.

  Necessity > Will.
