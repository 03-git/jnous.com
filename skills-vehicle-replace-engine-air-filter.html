REPLACE AN ENGINE AIR FILTER
Steps:
1. Locate air filter box (large plastic box near top of engine with intake hose).
2. Unclip or unscrew lid. Note orientation of old filter.
3. Remove old filter. Inspect housing for debris; wipe clean.
4. Insert new filter same orientation. Ensure edges seat flush.
5. Close lid. Reclip or rescrew. Confirm snug.
Notes:
- Replace per manual schedule. More often in dusty areas.
- A visibly dirty filter (held to light, no light passes) is overdue.
- Do not oil a dry filter. Oiled filters are for specific aftermarket setups only.
