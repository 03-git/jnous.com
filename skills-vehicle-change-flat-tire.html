CHANGE A FLAT TIRE
Steps:
1. Pull to firm, level surface away from traffic. Hazards on.
2. Apply parking brake. Chock opposite diagonal wheel.
3. Get spare, jack, lug wrench from trunk.
4. Loosen lug nuts 1/2 turn while tire is on the ground.
5. Place jack on vehicle's jack point (check manual for location). Raise until flat tire clears ground.
6. Remove lug nuts. Pull off flat tire.
7. Mount spare. Hand-thread lug nuts.
8. Lower vehicle until spare touches ground (slight weight on it).
9. Torque lug nuts in star pattern to spec.
10. Lower fully. Remove jack and chocks.
11. Check spare pressure. Drive to shop (compact spares: reduced speed, limited distance -- check label on spare).
Notes:
- Never get under a vehicle on a jack alone.
- Retorque lug nuts after driving a short distance.
- Compact spares are temporary. Replace with full-size tire promptly.
