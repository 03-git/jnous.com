VARIANCE LAB

       40+ models. 135k+ inferences. N=100. Cross-platform.
       Findings from running models locally and measuring what happens.

HIGHLIGHTS

       Passenger mode (human stops steering, model explores unconstrained):
       11% of sessions, 66% of tokens, 41x cost multiplier.

       Delegated execution across 3 nodes: 48% of the time, same cost.

       Qwen 0.5B: followed the rules 95.3% of the time at 1/3 the
       parameters of its bigger siblings.
