REPLACE A CAR BATTERY
Steps:
1. Engine off. Key out of ignition.
2. Disconnect NEGATIVE (−) cable first. Tuck it away from terminal.
3. Disconnect POSITIVE (+) cable.
4. Remove battery hold-down clamp (bolt or bracket).
5. Lift out old battery (30-50 lbs). Use both hands.
6. Clean terminal connectors with wire brush and baking soda paste.
7. Set new battery in tray. Same orientation (terminals match cable reach).
8. Install hold-down clamp. Snug, not gorilla-tight.
9. Connect POSITIVE (+) first. Then NEGATIVE (−).
10. Start vehicle. Reset clock/radio presets.
Notes:
- Negative first off, positive first on. This prevents shorts.
- Battery acid is sulfuric acid. Wear gloves and eye protection.
- Most auto parts stores test, sell, and recycle batteries.
- Some vehicles need a battery registration procedure (BMW, others). Check manual.
