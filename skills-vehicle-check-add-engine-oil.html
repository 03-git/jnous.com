CHECK AND ADD ENGINE OIL
Steps:
1. Park on level ground. Engine off 5+ minutes (oil settles).
2. Pull dipstick, wipe clean with lint-free rag.
3. Reinsert fully, pull again. Read level between MIN and MAX marks.
4. If low: remove oil filler cap on valve cover.
5. Add oil in 100ml increments. Wait 1 minute between adds.
6. Recheck dipstick. Stop when level is between MIN and MAX.
7. Replace filler cap and dipstick. Wipe spills.
Notes:
- Use oil weight from owner's manual (e.g. 5W-30). Do not mix weights.
- Dark oil is normal. Gritty or milky oil means service needed now.
- Overfilling causes foaming and engine damage. Add slowly.
